Beach Wedding Planning Tips

Expert advice for your perfect coastal celebration

Best Times to Wed

Plan your ceremony during golden hour (1 hour before sunset) for the most romantic lighting and comfortable temperatures.

Weather Considerations

Always have a backup plan for inclement weather. Consider wind patterns when choosing decorations and hairstyles.

Footwear & Attire

Choose appropriate footwear for sand. Consider barefoot ceremonies or wedge heels that won't sink into the sand.
